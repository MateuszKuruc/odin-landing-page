Welcome to Oktoberfest

Oktoberfest is the world's largest festival for true beer enthusiast. It is hosted in Munich each year and gathers between 5.5 and 7 million people in the span of 16 to 18 days. These are unbelievable numbers, but we are talking about beer after all!
