Welcome to Oktoberfest

Oktoberfest is the world's largest festival for true beer enthusiast. It is hosted in Munich each year and gathers between 5.5 and 7 million people in the span of 16 to 18 days. These are unbelievable numbers, but we are talking about beer after all!

What to expect at Oktoberfest?

First and foremost - beer! A whole range of beer types is available at hundreds of stands.
Lederhosen - a classic Bavarian outfit with characteristic shorts, socks and a hat!
No German festival can succeed without wurst that lets visitors fuel the whole days at Oktoberfest!
Crispy snacks between beers are universally loved addon worldwide!
